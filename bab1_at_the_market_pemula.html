Bab 1: At the Market

Tingkat: [Pemula]

BAGIAN 1: KOSA KATA PENTING

  • Market (noun): Pasar.
  • To buy (verb): Membeli.
  • Fresh (adjective): Segar.
  • Vegetable (noun): Sayuran.
  • Fruit (noun): Buah.
  • Money (noun): Uang.
  • Bag (noun): Tas atau kantong.
  • Red (adjective): Merah.
  • Happy (adjective): Senang.
  • Home (noun): Rumah.

BAGIAN 2: CERITA PENDEK

Today, I go to the market. The market is big.

I want to buy food. I see fresh vegetables. I see green spinach. I see orange carrots. I buy the vegetables. I put them in my bag.

Then, I see fruit. I see a big, red apple. I like apples. I buy the red apple.

I give money to the seller. I have vegetables and fruit in my bag. I am happy. I go home.
