Bab 2: My Family

Tingkat: [Pemula]

BAGIAN 1: KOSA KATA PENTING

  • Family (noun): Keluarga.
  • Father (noun): Ayah.
  • Mother (noun): Ibu.
  • Brother (noun): Saudara laki-laki.
  • Sister (noun): Saudara perempuan.
  • To live (verb): Tinggal.
  • House (noun): Rumah.
  • To love (verb): Mencintai; menyayangi.
  • Kind (adjective): Baik hati.

BAGIAN 2: CERITA PENDEK

This is my family. I love my family.

My father is tall. He is a doctor. My mother is very kind. She is a teacher. They love me.

I have one brother and one sister. My brother is named Budi. He likes to play football. My sister is named Ani. She likes to read books.

We live in a small house. Our house is yellow. We are a happy family.
