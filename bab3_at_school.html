Tingkat: [Menengah]

BAGIAN 1: KOSA KATA PENTING

  • Assignment (noun): Tugas (biasanya tugas akademis seperti esai atau proyek).
  • Deadline (noun): Tenggat waktu; batas waktu pengumpulan.
  • To collaborate (verb): Bekerja sama; berkolaborasi dalam sebuah tim.
  • To struggle (verb): Berjuang; mengalami kesulitan untuk melakukan sesuatu.
  • Overwhelmed (adjective): Kewalahan; merasa terbebani oleh terlalu banyak hal.
  • To encourage (verb): Mendorong; memberi semangat.
  • Presentation (noun): Presentasi; penyajian materi di depan audiens.
  • Confident (adjective): Percaya diri.
  • Research (noun): Penelitian; riset untuk mencari informasi.
  • Peer (noun): Teman sebaya; rekan.

BAGIAN 2: CERITA PENDEK

The History Project

The semester was getting busy. Last week, our history teacher gave us a new assignment. We had to work in groups to create a presentation about ancient civilizations. The deadline was only two weeks away, which made everyone feel a bit stressed.

I was grouped with two of my peers, Sarah and Doni. Initially, I felt completely overwhelmed. The topic was huge, and I had no idea where to start our research. For the first few days, we really began to struggle. We spent hours in the library, but the information we found was confusing.

Seeing our difficulty, our teacher, Mr. Adi, came to our table. He didn't give us the answers directly, but he did encourage us to change our approach. "Don't try to learn everything at once," he advised. "Divide the topic into smaller parts and collaborate on each one."

His advice was exactly what we needed. We decided that I would research the daily life, Sarah would focus on the government, and Doni would cover the architecture. We started to collaborate much more effectively. We shared our findings every afternoon and helped each other when one of us got stuck. The project that once seemed impossible started to take shape.

By the time the deadline arrived, we were not only ready, but we also felt very confident. Our presentation went smoothly. We learned a lot about history, but more importantly, we learned how to overcome a challenge by working together as a team.
